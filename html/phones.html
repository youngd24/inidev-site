Obtaining Telephone Service

All INITECH employees are provided with an internal telephone extension in the 5-digit format 5-xxxx. For most daily needs, this is sufficient. However, for those rare and privileged moments when external Direct Inward Dial (DID) numbers are required, please follow the procedure below:

  • Fill out Form 37-D/B and submit it to the Telephony Liaison Officer in triplicate.
  • Obtain 13 separate approvals from various department heads, legal, HR, and at least one facilities custodian.
  • Successfully bribe your closest Vice President (amounts vary, but pizza and compliments are a good start).

Lead Times: Estimated wait ranges from 5 to 500 business days, depending on:

  • How the IS Support Services team feels that week
  • Your behavior towards them over the past fiscal quarter
  • Whether you returned the last copy of "Installing Lotus Notes for Idiots"

NOTE: Voicemail is NOT available. IS Support has determined it is stupid, annoying, and not worth our precious time.
